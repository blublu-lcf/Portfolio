Introduction

Compared to nuclear families in Western countries, Chinese families often follow an extended structure where kids are often brought up by their grandparents. “Granny Blue” is an experimental film that abstractly tells the story of my changing relationship with my grandma throughout my growing up. This film combines psychological and optical theories of the color blue to convey my blue emotions toward my grandma, who was diagnosed with Alzheimer's a year ago. Although we are gradually separated by geographical, physical, and psychological distances which brings many feelings of blue, it remains a blessing to have our lives intersect.
