Introduction

Gravicam is a two-player interactive installation that popularizes the imaging principles of " gravitational lensing " through artistic and gamified cross-media forms. The X126 celestial body player alters their angle relative to the "lens object" and adjusts its mass to send light signals. The observer player can then capture artistic transformations of the X126 player's face as influenced by the gravitational lensing effect.
