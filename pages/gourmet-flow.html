Introduction

Gourmet Flow is a gamified smart product which engages the adolescents with AN in creating their own gourmet music with their senses of sight, smell, and taste during mindful eating. By utilizing Mixed Reality (MR) technology, AI models and smartphone-based APP , the product can turn eating into an enjoyable and artistic experience.
