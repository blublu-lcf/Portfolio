Introduction

Rose & Palette is an interactive narrative game which gamifies a private story from my high school life. I was an ordinary girl when a cool girl came into my class. At first, I was attracted by her and admired her deeply. I tried to imitate her but eventually became self-abased and completely lost myself. In the end, I managed to rediscover who I truly am. This game uses interactive gaming mechanism to invite players to empathize with my complex and subtle emotions, and try to heal every ordinary girl's heart who might share the same experience as me.
